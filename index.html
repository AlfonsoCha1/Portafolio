About Me

I’m a Computer Systems Engineer who enjoys being the person people can rely on when something isn’t working. I have hands-on experience in IT support and networking troubleshooting internet and connectivity issues, setting up computers and peripherals, and helping keep office environments running smoothly. I like fixing problems in a practical way: first I listen, then I test what’s happening, and I keep going until it’s solved. I’m pretty organized with how I work. I don’t like guessing. I prefer checking the basics, narrowing down the cause, and finding a solution that actually lasts. I also care about making things easier for others, whether that’s explaining a fix in a simple way or leaving things documented so it’s not the same issue again. On the developer side, I’m still growing, but I’m consistent. I keep learning and building personal projects because I genuinely enjoy it. I like working with Python and web technologies to create useful things, automate small tasks, and improve my skills little by little. I’m not trying to sound perfect. I just like learning, building, and getting better over time. In general, I work well both on my own and with a team. I’m easy to work with, I take responsibility, and I like getting things done the right way.
